I led a collaborative research with my team to collect and analyze data on Manchester City Football/Soccer games from 2018 to the present. This involved:

  1. Extracting information from the Google Football/Soccer Database using Beautiful Soup.
  2. Employing Pandas to clean the dataset thoroughly.
  3. Strategically sampling games to eliminate bias.
  4. Conducting a statistical analysis using the R programming language to explore the details of the dataset in depth.
  5. Developing a sophisticated predictive linear regression model as the culmination of our efforts.
  6. Fine-tuning the model through the strategic application of backward elimination techniques to ensure accurate forecasts of the total goals scored by Manchester City.
